# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to making participation in the tinyclaw community a harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment include:

- Being respectful and constructive
- Accepting feedback with curiosity
- Focusing critique on ideas and implementation
- Supporting newcomers and non-expert contributors

Examples of unacceptable behavior include harassment, discrimination, personal attacks, and publishing private information without consent.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing standards of acceptable behavior and may remove, edit, or reject contributions that do not align with this Code of Conduct.
